# Feature Evaluation

## Source Summarisation

Strengths:
- Accurate summary of uploaded content
- Fast identification of key concepts

Limitations:
- Depends on source quality

## Question Answering

Strengths:
- Context-aware responses
- References source material

Limitations:
- Users may accept answers without verification

## Study Guide Generation

Strengths:
- Useful for revision and exam preparation
- Creates structured learning material automatically

Limitations:
- May miss deeper context from source documents

## Audio Overview

Strengths:
- Supports alternative learning styles
- Improves accessibility

Limitations:
- Users may not verify information against source documents

## Overall Assessment

NotebookLM is suitable for educational support when combined with human oversight and critical review.
